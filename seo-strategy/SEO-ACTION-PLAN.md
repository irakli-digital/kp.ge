```

    │   ├── /models/chatgpt
    │   ├── /models/claude
    │   └── /models/gemini
    │
    ├── /tools/ (HUB) ← Articles #7-11
    │   ├── /tools/paraphraser
    │   ├── /tools/spellchecker
    │   ├── /tools/pdf-analyzer
    │   └── /tools/translator
    │
    └── /templates/ (HUB) ← Articles #12-16
        ├── /templates/cv-maker
        └── /templates/cover-letter
```

### Anchor Text Distribution
- **70%** Exact Match (Georgian): "ტექსტის გადაკეთება", "შექმენით CV"
- **20%** Action-based: "სცადეთ უფასოდ", "დაიწყეთ აქ"
- **10%** Navigational: "Mypen Paraphraser", "ჩვენი ინსტრუმენტი"

### Cross-Linking Rules
1. Each article links to its **Hub page** (primary internal link)
2. Each article links to **2-3 related articles** within the same silo
3. Battle articles (#1, #4, #5, #6) cross-link to each other
4. Career articles (#12-16) form a tight cluster
5. Education articles (#17-20) link to PDF tools (#9)
